that means the optimal policy is that if we follow this policy, we can get a sequences of states and it's corresponding rewards sum is max. Also the rewards is discounted by γ\gammaγ factor.

Next how to express the utility of s:
Uπ(s)=E[∑t=0∞γtR(st)∣π,s0=s]U^{\pi}(s) = E[\sum_{t=0}^{\infin}\gamma^{t}R(s_{t})|\pi, s_{0} = s]Uπ(s)=E[∑t=0∞​γtR(st​)∣π,s0​=s]
